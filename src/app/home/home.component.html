Manitoba Council of Administrative Tribunals

Our Goals and Undertakings

Facilitate Sharing

To facilitate the sharing of professional information, experience and expertise from within the membership.

Promote Education

To promote and assist in the education and training of tribunal members and staff.

Address Common Concerns

To address common concerns of members within the administrative justice system in Manitoba.

Consultation for Government

To act as a source of information and consultation for the Government of Manitoba on issues relating to the administration, development and improvement of the administrative justice system.

Liaise with Other Jurisdictions

To liaise with tribunal members and staff in other provinces and in the federal jurisdiction and with their organizations.
