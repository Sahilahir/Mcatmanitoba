About

The Manitoba Council of Administrative Tribunals (MCAT) is a provincial organization dedicated to promoting administrative justice and supporting the work of administrative tribunals.

Our Mission

  • Enhancing and expanding connections among members of the administrative justice community.
  • Promoting awareness of the role and importance of administrative justice in everyday life.
  • Advocating on key issues that impact the administrative justice sector.
  • Providing valuable support and services to our members.

We strive to keep our members informed about emerging issues through forums for discussion, education, research, and policy development across all fields of administrative justice.
